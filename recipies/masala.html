Recipie for Chiken Tikka Masala Rice

Description

Chicken tikka masala rice is a delicious and popular dish that combines two classic Indian recipes: chicken tikka masala and rice. It typically consists of succulent pieces of chicken, marinated in a yogurt and spice mixture, which are then grilled or roasted until tender and slightly charred. The chicken is then simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce, infused with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. This flavorful sauce is usually creamy and slightly tangy, with a hint of smokiness. To complete the dish, the chicken tikka masala is served on a bed of fluffy basmati rice. The rice is often cooked separately and seasoned with spices like cumin seeds, cardamom pods, and cinnamon for added fragrance and taste. The combination of tender chicken tikka, rich and creamy tomato sauce, and aromatic rice creates a satisfying and well-balanced meal with layers of flavors. Chicken tikka masala rice is a popular choice in Indian restaurants and takeaways, and it has gained popularity worldwide as a beloved fusion dish. It offers a delightful blend of textures, colors, and tastes, making it a favorite among those who appreciate the vibrant and diverse flavors of Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450 grams) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
  • 3 cups cooked basmati rice

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, paprika, garam masala, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well with the marinade. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 1 hour, or refrigerate overnight for best results.
  2. Preheat your grill or oven to medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken until it is cooked through and slightly charred, about 8-10 minutes per side. Set aside. In a large skillet or pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until it turns golden brown, about 5 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes. Add the diced tomatoes to the pan and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Then, using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the tomato mixture until smooth. Stir in the heavy cream and cooked chicken tikka pieces into the tomato sauce. Allow the mixture to simmer over low heat for 10-15 minutes, until the flavors meld together. In the meantime, reheat the cooked basmati rice. You can do this by steaming it for a few minutes or warming it in the microwave. To serve, place a portion of basmati rice on a plate or bowl and ladle the chicken tikka masala sauce over it. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
